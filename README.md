In this story , we are having a child dyslexic kid with dyslexia. He has serious problems  with reading and writing. Also he cannot express his emotions and feelings by telling them. Thus he feels very unwanted in his own family. His parents are unaware about his disease. In school he cannot be responsive and he has no friends with whom he can play. Teachers are unable to catch his problems and thus he felt abandoned in school too.

One day a teacher named Amir joins his school as a drawing teacher. He is very friendly and humble with his students. Somehow he noticed that dyslexic kid is not responding like other childs. This hits him and he tries to talk with dyslexic kid but he(dyslexic kid) doesn’t respond to him.

Then one day he goes to dyslexic kid during the class and checks his copies. He identifies that dyslexic kid’s writings are in jumbled words and also dyslexic kid cannot read properly. After researching on that, Amir finds that dyslexic kid has a disease called Dyslexia. 

Amir decides that he will help dyslexic kid to get rid from dyslexia and help him to lead a healthy and happy life like other children.

Making some decisions Amir will help dyslexic kid to express his feelings without using words to speak. After selecting choices in the game, Amir finally gets that dyslexic kid will speak through colors. Also he invents that this dyslexic kid has a big talent for drawing. Which states that Speak without words but through the colors. This is how a dyslexic kid can overcome his obstacles.

To add Dialogic to your project and solve the error, follow these steps:
> Install & Enable

> Restart: Close and reopen Godot to ensure the DialogicStyle error clears.
